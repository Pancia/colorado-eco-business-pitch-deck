The Vision

A place where driven men come together to build, train, and grow alongside each other in a powerful natural environment.

The Summit is the foundation of something real. Bimonthly residencies will bring selected members out to Colorado to network with like-minded men, train together physically and mentally, help develop the land and the structures on it, and live the lifestyle we are creating.

Long-term, we will offer sustainable housing to those who prove to be a strong fit for the vision and the community. This is not a retreat center. It is a village.

The Lineage

Why Men's Academy. Why Now.

This project comes out of The Men's Academy — a brotherhood led by Kristian Bell of Wisdom Warriors. The men here are already doing the work. The Summit is the next step: a shared place to do it on.

We are not looking for capital detached from the mission. We are looking for partners who recognize what is being built and want to stand behind it — and inside it.

Business Model

Four revenue streams, one village.

01

Bimonthly Residencies

Week-long cohorts. 8 men. Training, building, brotherhood. Primary revenue engine in year one.

02

Long-Term Housing

Sustainable cabins / structures for members who have earned their place. Monthly or yearly terms.

03

Work-Trade

Reduced or waived residency fees in exchange for labor building out the land and structures.

04

Partnerships

Aligned collaborations with men's work, health, and outdoor brands — co-hosted retreats and sponsorships.
