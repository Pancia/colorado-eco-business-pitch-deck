The Promise

The man you leave as is not the man who arrived.

You will sweat. You will build something with your hands. You will sit at fire with men who will tell you the truth. You will leave with a body that's stronger, a mind that's sharper, and a circle of men who know your name.

This is not a retreat. It is a forging.

The Arc

Seven days. A named rite.

Day 1–2

Arrival & Council

Drop the week behind you. Meet the men. State why you came. The first fire.

Day 3–5

The Work

Ice bath at 5am. Framing and land work by noon. Training in the afternoon. Council at dusk.

Day 6–7

The Integration

What you take home. Commitments made to the cohort. The last fire. Departure.

The Land

Mountains. Trees. Silence.

Rural ski country in southwestern Colorado. Skiing in winter. Hiking, training, building, chopping wood the rest of the year. The land itself does most of the work.

Who this is for

And who it isn't.

The Summit is for men who are already on the path — training, building businesses, raising families, doing the inner work — and want to do it alongside other men who are doing the same. Men's Academy alignment is a strong signal.

It is not a first stop. If you're looking for a retreat, this isn't it. If you're looking for something to push against, welcome.
